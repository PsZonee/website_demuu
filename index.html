Frequently Asked Questions

❓ Is my identity kept confidential?

Yes. You can report anonymously. Your personal details are encrypted and shared only with authorized investigators.

❓ What happens after I file a complaint?

Your complaint is assigned a unique Reference ID. The cybercrime unit reviews it and may contact you for further details within 48 hours.

❓ Can I report on behalf of someone else?

Yes. You can file a complaint as a witness or on behalf of a family member. Provide their details in the description.

❓ Is there a time limit to report?

Report as soon as possible. For financial fraud, immediate reporting (within 1 hour) increases the chance of fund recovery.
